Admissions Process

Step 1: Make an Enquiry

Begin by completing our online enquiry form or contacting our admissions office. We'll provide you with detailed information about our programmes, fees, and schedule a personalized school tour.

Our admissions team is available to answer all your questions and help you understand what makes Vivflourish unique.

Step 2: School Tour & Assessment

Visit our campus to experience our learning environment firsthand. Your child will complete a friendly assessment to help us understand their current level and learning needs.

Tours are available weekdays from 9am to 2pm. We recommend allowing 1-2 hours for the complete tour and assessment process.

Step 3: Registration

Once admission is offered, complete the registration process by submitting required documents and paying the registration fee. We'll guide you through every step.

Required documents include birth certificate, previous school reports, immunization records, and passport photographs.

Step 4: Resumption

Welcome to the Vivflourish family! Before resumption, you'll receive an orientation package including school calendar, uniform requirements, book lists, and other essential information.

We host a New Parents Orientation to ensure a smooth transition for both students and families.

Activities & Excursions

We believe learning extends beyond the classroom. Our carefully planned excursions connect classroom knowledge with real-world experiences, fostering cultural awareness and historical understanding.

Historical & Cultural Sites

Our students explore Nigeria's rich history through visits to significant sites:

  • Badagry Slave Trade House: Understanding history and resilience
  • National Museum: Appreciating Nigeria's cultural heritage
  • First Storey Building in Nigeria: Exploring architectural history
  • Point of No Return: Reflecting on historical journeys

Vlek Conservation Centre

Our annual visit to Vlek Conservation Centre provides hands-on environmental education:

  • Ecosystem exploration and biodiversity studies
  • Wildlife conservation awareness
  • Environmental sustainability workshops
  • Outdoor team-building activities
  • Nature-inspired art and creativity sessions

Additional Enrichment Activities

Beyond excursions, we offer a wide range of activities to develop diverse skills:

  • Annual Cultural Day celebrating Nigeria's diversity
  • Science and Innovation Fairs
  • Sports competitions and inter-house games
  • Music and drama productions
  • Community service projects
  • Entrepreneurship market days
